Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students
✅ Do’s
Think before posting; ensure your content is respectful and positive.
Use professional language when communicating online.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
Protect your privacy by using strong passwords and adjusting security settings.
Use social media to build a professional network and showcase achievements responsibly.
❌ Don’ts
Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or discriminatory content.
Do not engage in online harassment or cyberbullying.
Avoid spreading rumors or unverified news.
Do not post anything that could harm your academic or professional reputation.
