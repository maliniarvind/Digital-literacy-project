Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students
Use strong, unique passwords for all online accounts and change them regularly.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on emails, social media, and banking apps.
Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages.
Verify the sender before sharing personal or financial information online.
Never share UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank. (Financial Safety)
Use official banking apps or websites for transactions; avoid links from messages. (Financial Safety)
Keep software and antivirus programs updated on your phone and laptop.
Be careful with public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into bank or sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
Limit personal information shared on social media to reduce identity theft risks.
Report cybercrime immediately if you fall victim:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
